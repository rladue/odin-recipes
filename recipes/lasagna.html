Lasagna

Lasagna is a type of pasta, possibly one of the oldest types, made of very wide, flat sheets. Either term can also refer to an Italian dish made of stacked layers of lasagna alternating with fillings such as ragù, béchamel sauce, vegetables, cheeses, and seasonings and spices.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onions
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 (28 oucnce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6.5 oucnce) cans canned tomato sauce
  • 3 (6 oucnce) cans tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 Tbs white sugar
  • 4 Tbs chopped fresh parsely, divided
  • 1.5 tsp dried basil leaves
  • 1.5 tsp salt, divided or to taste
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 tsp tsp fennel seeds
  • 1/4 tsp ground black pepper
  • 12 Lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 pound mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Gather all of your ingredients.
  2. Cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic in a Dutch oven over medium heat until well browned.
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and water. Season with sugar, 2 Tbs parsely, basil, 1 tsp salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and pepper. Simmer covered, for about 1.5 hours, stirring occasionally.
  4. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8-10 minutes. Drain noodles, and rinse with cold water.
  5. In a mixing bow, combine ricotta cheese with egg, remaining 2 Tbs parsley and 1/2 tsp salt.
  6. Preheat over to 375 degrees F (190 C)
  7. To Assemble
    1. Spread 1.5 cups of meat sauce in bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
    2. Arrange six noodles lengthwise over the meat sauce.
    3. Spread 1/2 of the ricotta cheese mixture.
    4. Top with 1/3 of the mozzarella cheese slices.
    5. Spoon 1.5 cups of meat sauce over mozzarella and sprinkle with 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese.
  8. Repeat layers and top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Cover with foil: to prevent sticking, either spray foil with cooking spray, or make sure the foil does not touch the cheese.
  9. Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes.
  10. Rest lasagna for 15 minutres before serving.
